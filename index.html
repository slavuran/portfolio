THE HAUNTED HOUSE (1908) // COMD282

This project's required task was to create a silent movie that belongs to the era of the 20th century. Challenge was the add sounds, dialogs, and music into the silent movie and create a new type of narrative. There were no sounds while I started to work on it. All the sound effects, music, and dialogs are added by me. This was one of my sound design and editing projects.

COMD 282// ENCHANTED(2007)

I aimed to create a recut trailer from a movie. The required task was to create a different genre than the original movie has. I thought about the completely different genres that I can arrange from musical comedy to horror movies. This was the final result of my editing.

You Have a Delivery// COMD282-Project02
